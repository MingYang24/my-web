works > concert pieces > Cross Road

Cross Road is a chamber music for ensemble and two Chinese instruments, Sheng and Pipa. The piece is inspired from the of sound at the cross road where I was listening all kinds of the noise, the takings, the horn, the cellphone ring.... Based on the ideal of soundscape by Pierre Scheaffer, the raw material of the piece is the pure imitation from the horn sound of automobile and bicycles, which construct the motive of an alternative intervals on major second and major third. Moreover, two parts of the piece, the first part of the sound from outside---- the sound of the street, mixing the second part of the sound inside, represents an experience of the bustle without and the turmoil within.
